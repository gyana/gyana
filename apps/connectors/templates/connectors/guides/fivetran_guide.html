Setting up a {{ service.name }} integration

{{ service.name }} integrations are simple to set up, we will guide you through a couple of steps to get your {{ service.name }} data into Gyana.

We've partnered with Fivetran to make the {{ servie.name }} integration with your data into Gyana as easy and frictionless as possible.

Step 1

After filling out the name you will be redirected to Fivetran.

Step 2

The Fivetran page will guide you through the steps to get your {{ service.name }} connected to Gyana. Afterwards you will be redirected back to Gyana.

Step 3

Back in Gyana the tables to be imported will have to be selected. Once you are happy with the selection of tables clicking on Select tables will finalize the setup.

Note: You can always change the selection of tables later on. You can find the setup on the Schema tab.
