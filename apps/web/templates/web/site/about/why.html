We built Gyana to save you time

Only 0.4% of the world can code. The rest of us struggle against the limitations of traditional business intelligence tools, or wait for weeks on the engineering team. It's frustrating and stops real work happening.

That's why we built Gyana. Gyana is designed for making custom1 dashboards, for any2 data source, without writing code.

You get the flexibility of code, with the usability of traditional business intelligence. Whatever dashboard you want to build, you can build it with Gyana, without waiting on someone else.

1 actually custom 2 yes, we really mean any
