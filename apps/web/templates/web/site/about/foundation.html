Our foundation

Gyana is a non-profit

Gyana was founded by Oxford University graduates to make it easier for people to work with data. After 7 years of pivots, triumphs and heartbreak, we decided to shutdown our for-profit company.

In March 2023, with the phenomenal support of our community and investors, we setup the Gyana Software Foundation and released Gyana under the permissive MIT license.

The goal of the foundation is to foster the development of open source, no-code technology. All paid plan revenue is spent on running Gyana cloud and furthering our mission.
